    From first home to mortgage refinancing - we're here for you at every step

    First home mortgage consulting

    Buying your first home is one of the most significant and exciting steps in life. We understand that the process can seem complex and intimidating, which is why we offer comprehensive first home mortgage consulting that accompanies you from start to finish.

    Our service includes a detailed explanation of all available mortgage types, checking your eligibility for mortgages and government benefits, and managing interest rate tenders between different banks. We'll help you understand all required documents and accompany you to every bank meeting.

    As part of our mortgage consulting, we'll build a detailed financial plan for you that shows exactly how much you can afford, what the expected monthly payment will be, and how the mortgage will affect your financial situation over the years. Our goal is to ensure you start life in your new home with peace of mind and financial security.

    Our advantages:

    • Comprehensive explanation of all mortgage types
    • Eligibility check for government benefits
    • Interest rate bidding management across all banks
    • Personal guidance at every stage of the process
    • Detailed and personalized financial plan

    Mortgage refinancing and terms improvement

    If you already have a mortgage, you might be paying too much! The mortgage market is constantly changing, and interest rates that were competitive a few years ago may be too high today. Mortgage refinancing and terms improvement can save you thousands of shekels per year.

    We specialize in comprehensive review of your existing mortgage and identifying savings opportunities. We'll examine the interest rate you're currently paying, your mortgage terms, and various improvement options. Then, we'll approach all relevant banks and financial institutions and find the best offer for you.

    The mortgage refinancing process also includes checking upfront fees, calculating expected long-term savings, and ensuring the switch is truly worthwhile. We'll handle all the small details and ensure the process goes smoothly without unpleasant surprises. Our mortgage consulting guarantees you'll receive the best possible terms.

    What the service includes:

    • Comprehensive examination of existing mortgage
    • Identification of significant savings opportunities
    • Negotiation management with banks for improved terms
    • Accurate calculation of expected savings
    • Handling all bureaucracy and documentation

    Mortgage consulting for home upgraders

    Has your family grown and it's time to move to a larger home? Housing improvement is a significant step that requires careful financial planning. We provide mortgage consulting for housing upgraders that takes into account your existing mortgage and finds the optimal solution for the transition.

    As part of our mortgage consulting, we'll examine your current mortgage, assess the value of your existing property, and explore various options for financing the new apartment. Should you sell your current apartment first? Is it better to keep it and rent it out? We'll help you make the right decision based on your financial situation and goals.

    We'll also coordinate between selling the old property and purchasing the new one, check bridge mortgage options if needed, and ensure the transition occurs smoothly without excessive financial stress. Our goal is to help you upgrade your housing while maintaining financial stability and minimizing risks.

    How we help:

    • Examination of existing mortgage and financing options
    • Property valuation and financial capacity assessment
    • Consulting on selling vs. renting existing property
    • Coordination between sale and purchase
    • Checking bridge mortgage options

    Precise and personal mortgage matching

    Every person is unique, and so are their financial needs. We believe there's no one-size-fits-all solution, which is why we offer precise and personalized mortgage matching services. We'll build a mortgage that fits exactly your needs, financial situation, and future plans.

    The process begins with an in-depth consultation where we learn about your financial situation, income and expenses, future plans, and financial goals. Based on this information, we build a personalized mortgage plan that takes into account all relevant parameters.

    What the service includes:

    • In-depth and personal consultation meeting
    • Comprehensive analysis of financial situation
    • Building a personalized mortgage plan
    • Selection of optimal tracks
    • Professional recommendations based on experience

    Family financial consulting

    Proper management of family finances is the key to financial stability and achieving long-term financial goals. We offer comprehensive financial consulting that helps you manage your family budget smartly and efficiently.

    We'll start by building a complete picture of your financial situation - income, fixed and variable expenses, debts and financial obligations. We'll identify savings opportunities and build a customized budget plan that will help you achieve your financial goals.

    What we'll do together:

    Pension portfolio review

    Your pension portfolio is one of your most important assets and will significantly impact your quality of life in retirement. Despite this, many of us don't know what's in our pension portfolio or whether it's managed optimally.

    We offer a comprehensive examination service for your pension portfolio. We'll check the funds and tracks you're investing in, examine their performance over time, and compare them to market alternatives. We'll also check the fees you're paying and ensure they're reasonable.

    Based on our examination, we'll provide professional recommendations for improving your pension portfolio. This may include transferring to better-performing funds, changing investment tracks, reducing fees, and more. The goal is to maximize returns on your pension savings and ensure a comfortable and dignified retirement.

    What the service includes:

    • Comprehensive examination of existing pension portfolio
    • Performance analysis and market comparison
    • Fee and expense examination
    • Recommendations for portfolio improvement
    • Guidance through the transfer and modification process
