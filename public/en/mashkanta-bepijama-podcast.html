    Why Mortgage in Pajamas?

    The world of mortgages is often perceived as intimidating, complex, and full of terms that are foreign to the average person. Interest rates, indices, amortization schedules, linked and unlinked tracks – all of these create great confusion among young couples and investors alike. The goal of "Mortgage in Pajamas" is to take this heavy subject and make it accessible in the most light, clear, and comfortable way possible – just like sitting in the living room in pajamas and chatting with a friend who understands the business.

    In each episode, we dive deep into burning issues in the real estate and finance field. Whether you're looking for mortgage advice before purchasing your first apartment, or you're experienced investors interested in optimizing your property portfolio, this podcast was created for you. We believe that knowledge is power, and the more you know, the more informed decisions you can make that will save you hundreds of thousands of shekels over the life of your mortgage.

    Ariel Achon brings to the table years of experience and deep familiarity with the banking system, breaking down the fine print that banks don't always want you to understand. This is not just another dry information channel, but a dynamic, current, and important conversation for every household in Israel.

    What do we talk about?

    A taste of the main topics covered in the podcast episodes

    First Home with Peace of Mind

    The process of purchasing your first home is both the most exciting and frightening. We explain the importance of mortgage advice for first-time buyers, how to build the right mix, and what are the common mistakes of young couples.

    Mortgage Refinancing and Savings

    Is your mortgage still relevant? We discuss changing market conditions, when it's worthwhile to refinance and improve terms, and how you can shorten the loan period or reduce monthly payments.

    Home Upgraders and Investors

    For those already in the real estate game, mortgage advice for home upgraders is critical. We talk about mortgage portability, leveraging existing properties, and bridge loans.

    Listening That's an Investment in Your Future

    Many ask me, "Why listen to a podcast if I'm taking an advisor anyway?". The answer is simple: a client who understands is a client who profits more. When you approach the mortgage advisory process with basic knowledge and understanding of the concepts, the dialogue becomes much more productive. The podcast allows you to familiarize yourself with the terminology, understand the various considerations, and arrive better prepared for the meeting with the bank or advisor.

    In the various episodes of "Mortgage in Pajamas," we sometimes host experts from related fields – lawyers, appraisers, and economists – to give you a complete 360-degree picture of the biggest transaction of your life. We analyze the impact of the Consumer Price Index on monthly payments, examine what happens when the prime rate rises, and explain why the lowest interest rate is not always the cheapest mortgage.
